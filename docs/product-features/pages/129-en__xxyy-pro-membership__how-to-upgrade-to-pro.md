# How to Upgrade to Pro

To view your current point balance, navigate to the user menu in the top-right corner and select 'Membership Points'.

* How Points Are Earned\
  Trading Points are calculated based on the combined activity across all of your trading wallets. This includes both the total number of trades (buys and sells) and the overall volume of those trades.
* When Points Are Distributed\
  Your earned points are automatically airdropped to your account every day at 00:00 UTC.
