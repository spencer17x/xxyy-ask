# Generate Your Trading Wallet

For enhanced security and a seamless experience, XXYY uses a dedicated wallet for all transactions. After your first login, you will need to create at least one trading wallet to get started.

1\. Generate Your Wallet\
From the navigation bar, select the blockchain you wish to trade on and click the "Generate Wallet" button.

2\. Secure Your Private Key (CRITICAL STEP)\
After your wallet is created, you will see its public address. The private key will remain hidden until you click "View Private Key".

* IMPORTANT: For your security, the private key for each wallet is displayed only one time. You must immediately copy and save it in a secure, offline location (like a password manager or physical document).
* XXYY does not store your private keys and cannot recover them for you if they are lost. Losing your private key will result in the permanent loss of your assets in that wallet.

3\. Fund Your Wallet\
To begin trading, transfer assets to your new trading wallet. You can do this by copying the wallet's public address or by scanning its QR code from another wallet or exchange. Once the funds arrive, you're ready to trade.
