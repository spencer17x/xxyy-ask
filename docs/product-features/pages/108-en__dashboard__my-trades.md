# My trades

The My Trades section provides a detailed, chronological log of every transaction you have made for the current token with your selected wallet.

This detailed history allows you to review each individual trade and understand precisely how your overall P\&L is calculated. The transaction log includes the following columns:

* Date: The date and time the transaction was executed.
* Type: The type of trade (e.g., Buy or Sell).
* Price (SOL): The price per token in SOL at the time of the transaction.
* Total (USD): The total value of the trade in U.S. Dollars.
* Total (SOL): The total value of the trade in SOL.
* Makers: Displays specific on-chain participant data for the trade.
* Action: Provides a direct link to view the full transaction details on a block explorer like Solscan.
