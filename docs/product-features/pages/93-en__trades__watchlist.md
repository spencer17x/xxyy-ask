# Watchlist

The Watchlist is your personal dashboard for monitoring tokens of interest. It allows you to track key metrics like current price, market cap, and 24-hour performance. To quickly identify market movers, you can sort the entire list by either 1-hour or 24-hour price change.

Adding Tokens and Setting Notes

To add a token to your watchlist, simply click on it in the project information area. You will be prompted to either assign it to an existing group or create a new one using the "create group" option.

Once a token is on your watchlist, you can select it and use the "Set remark" function to add a personal note. This is a perfect way to remember your strategy, such as "Why I originally bought this token" or "Target sell price is $X."

Group Management and Organization

Your watchlist can be filtered and sorted by your custom groups. For advanced organization, you can enter the "management group" section.

When viewing tokens within a specific group, you have two distinct options:

* Remove from Group: This action only removes the token from the current group you are viewing. The token will remain on your main watchlist.
* Remove from Watchlist (Un-watch): This action completely removes the token from your watchlist and all associated groups.
