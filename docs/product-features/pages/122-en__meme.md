# Meme

he main discovery view is divided into a top control bar for your trading settings and three columns that track tokens at different stages of their lifecycle.

Trade Settings Bar

This bar at the top of the screen displays your current default trading parameters and allows for quick adjustments. It shows:

* Your active Trading Mode (e.g., Anti-MEV).
* Your current Slippage Tolerance (e.g., 0%).
* Your current Priority Fee (e.g., 0).
* A settings icon (⚙️) for more detailed configuration.
