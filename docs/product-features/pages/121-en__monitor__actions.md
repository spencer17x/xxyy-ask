# Actions

For each wallet on your list, the Actions column on the right-hand side provides several icons for quick management.

* Pause Alerts: Click the pause icon to temporarily stop receiving push notifications for this wallet's trading activity. The wallet will remain on your list.
* Edit: Click the edit icon to open the settings window and modify the monitoring configuration for this address.
* Unfollow: Click the unfollow icon to permanently remove this wallet from your monitoring list.
