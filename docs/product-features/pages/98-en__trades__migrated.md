# Migrated

The Migrated section tracks projects during the critical transition phase from their initial launch protocol to a live market on a DEX. This list includes projects that are currently in the process of migrating as well as those that have just recently completed the process.

The feed is sorted in reverse chronological order, meaning the projects that are actively migrating or have just finished migrating will always appear at the top.

By monitoring this view, you can research a project's background and prepare to act. It provides the perfect opportunity to execute a timely launch snipe or an immediate swap the moment the token becomes fully tradable on the open market.
