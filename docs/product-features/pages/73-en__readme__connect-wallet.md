# Connect Wallet

To get started, please ensure you have a compatible wallet installed on your device.

Recommended Wallets

* On Desktop: Phantom, OKX Web3 Wallet
* On Mobile: Phantom, OKX Web3 Wallet

Once your wallet is installed, simply click the "Connect Wallet" button on our website and approve the connection prompt within your wallet. You'll then be logged in and ready to trade.
