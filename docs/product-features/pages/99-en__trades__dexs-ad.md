# DEXs-AD

The Dexs-AD feed lists tokens from projects that have purchased advertising space on the popular charting platform, Dexscreener.

This list is sorted in reverse chronological order based on the time the ad was purchased, ensuring that the projects with the most recent ad placements always appear at the top.
