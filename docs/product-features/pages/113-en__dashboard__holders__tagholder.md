# TagHolder

The Tag Holders view is a powerful filter that cross-references your personal monitored list with the holders of the current token. It instantly shows you which of the 'smart money' or KOL wallets you follow are invested in this project.

This allows you to quickly see their current position and past activity, helping you make faster, more informed trading decisions based on the actions of wallets you trust.

Important Note: If a wallet from your monitored list appears in this view with a holding amount of zero (or near-zero), it signifies that they have sold their entire position. This historical data is crucial, as it shows you that a trusted wallet once held the token but has since exited.
