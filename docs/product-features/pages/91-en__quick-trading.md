# Quick Trading

XXYY includes a Quick Trading panel, allowing you to buy or sell with a single click. To activate it, click the floating icon on the right side of the screen. The panel can then be dragged and positioned anywhere on your page for easy access.

Configuring the Quick Trading Panel

To customize the panel, click the settings icon in its top-right corner. Here, you can define the preset amounts for each buy and sell button, as well as their specific slippage and fee settings.

Please Note: The Trading Mode (e.g., Anti-MEV, Turbo) selected in the main, right-side trade panel is directly linked to the Quick Trading panel. This means your selected mode, along with its associated slippage and fee settings, will automatically apply to any trade you make using the quick buttons.
