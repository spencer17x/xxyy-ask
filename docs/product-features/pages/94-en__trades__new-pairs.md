# New Pairs

The New Pairs section is your real-time feed for discovering the very latest tokens the moment they are created. It automatically pulls in projects from a wide variety of launch platforms, including Pump,Moonshot,Bags and many others, with the newest tokens always appearing first.

For each project, you can instantly view key metrics like its market cap, time since creation, and its current launch progress (e.g., bonding curve completion). Clicking on any project will take you to its detailed K-line chart and information page, allowing you to quickly assess potential trading opportunities.

Sorting Options

The feed provides two powerful ways to organize the list:

* By Launch Time: This is the default view, sorting the list chronologically to show the absolute newest tokens at the top.
* By Progress: This mode prioritizes tokens that are closest to completing their launch goal (like a full bonding curve). It’s the perfect way to identify projects that are on the verge of migrating to a DEX like Raydium.
