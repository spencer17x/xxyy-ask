# Final Stretch

The Final Stretch view is your sniper's scope for discovering imminent trading opportunities. It isolates projects from across all supported launch protocols that are nearing the completion of their initial funding goal (such as a full bonding curve).

This list is automatically sorted by progress, placing the tokens closest to launching at the very top. By monitoring this section, you can identify and prepare for tokens that are about to migrate to a live market on a DEX like Raydium, giving you a crucial time advantage.
