# Referral Program

How It Works\
When you register on XXYY, a unique referral link is automatically generated for you. This link contains a customizable referral code.

* You can personalize your referral code one time only. Once changed, it cannot be modified again.
* Invite friends using your link. When they sign up and trade on XXYY, you will earn 10% of the trading fees they pay, paid out as a commission to you.

Track & Withdraw Your Rewards\
You can monitor your cumulative referral earnings and view your complete withdrawal history at any time.

* Withdrawing Commissions: You can request to withdraw your earned rewards. Approved withdrawals will be transferred to your selected trading wallet by 00:00 UTC on the following day.

Please Note:\
A user account can only be bound to one referral link. If an account has been used on XXYY before, it cannot be linked to a referrer or switched to a different referral link later.
