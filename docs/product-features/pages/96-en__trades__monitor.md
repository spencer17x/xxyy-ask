# Monitor

The Monitor is one of XXYY's standout features, giving you a strategic edge by allowing you to track the real-time trading activity and history of "smart money," KOLs, or any other wallet you choose to follow.

To start, click the "+ Monitor Wallet" button. This will open the setup window where you can configure your tracking settings.

Setting Up a Monitored Wallet

In the Monitor Wallet window, you will need to provide the following information:

* Wallet Address: The public address of the wallet you wish to track.
* Wallet Name: A custom name or label for the wallet so you can easily identify it.
* Set Wallet Group: Assign the wallet to a specific group for better organization.

You can also Customize Txn Alerts to receive notifications for specific activities, such as Buy, Sell, Send SOL, and more. Further refine your alerts by setting a Min Buy Amount or Min Sell Amount to only be notified about significant trades.

Analyzing the Transaction Feed

Once a wallet is being monitored, its transactions will appear in a live feed. Each entry provides rich details, including:

* Transaction time
* The custom Wallet Name you assigned
* The specific token that was bought or sold
* The exact amount and value of the transaction
* The token's market cap at the time of the trade

The Monitor intelligently categorizes each action, showing whether a wallet is opening a new position, adding to a position, reducing a position, or closing a position entirely.

Interactive On-Chart Analysis

The Monitor provides deep, visual analysis directly on the price chart. When viewing a specific token:

1. Clicking on a wallet's name in the transaction feed will instantly filter the list to show only that wallet's trade history for the current token.
2. Simultaneously, visual markers for every single one of that wallet's buys and sells are plotted directly onto the K-line chart. This allows you to precisely visualize their entry and exit points to inform your own trading decisions.
