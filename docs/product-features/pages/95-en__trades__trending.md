# Trending

The Trending section is one of XXYY's standout features, designed to give you a powerful market edge by showing you where the momentum is in real-time.

Our proprietary smart algorithm calculates a "trend score" for tokens, highlighting those generating the most activity and attention. You can analyze these trends across five different time intervals: 1 minute, 5 minutes, 1 hour, 6 hours, and 24 hours.

Sorting Options

You have two primary ways to organize the Trending list:

1. By Trend Score (Default): This automatically ranks tokens using our smart algorithm, placing the projects with the highest trend heat at the top.
2. By 24h Price Change: This allows you to sort tokens based purely on their price performance over the last 24 hours.

Filter by Protocol

To precisely target your search, you can filter the Trending list to show tokens from the specific protocols you are interested in. You can select a single protocol or combine multiple protocols in your filter. This gives you complete control over the data you see.

To help you quickly identify a token's origin, projects from specific protocols (like pump) will be marked with their corresponding logo next to the token name.
