# Wallet Management

XXYY allows you to create and manage multiple trading wallets for each blockchain.

* Standard users can create up to 100 trading wallets per chain.
* Pro users can create up to 500 trading wallets per chain.

After you Generate Wallet, it will appear in your Wallet Management list. From this list, you can pin your most frequently used wallets. Pinned wallets will be prioritized and displayed first in the wallet selection menu when you are trading.

Fund Transfers

* Deposit (Transfer In)\
  The Deposit function allows you to quickly transfer funds from your main connected wallet (Login Wallet) to any of your generated Trading Wallets. After submitting a deposit, you can track its status in your transfer history.
* Withdraw (Transfer Out)\
  The Withdraw function allows you to quickly transfer funds from any of your Trading Wallets back to your main connected wallet (Login Wallet). After submitting a withdrawal, you can track its status in your transfer history.
